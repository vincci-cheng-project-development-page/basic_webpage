Environment

This portion of the website briefly talks about environmental issues in the urban context and proposes some strategies to address them.


    Ways to Save Electricity in the Household


  • Turn off the lights when not in use
  • Try to not use the heater and air-conditioner as often
  • Use energy-efficient lightbulbs
  • Limit the amount of food waste

Nature is so beautiful, it is our responsbility to protect it...


Reduce

It is important to reduce the amount of waste that we are producing. One example is to use refillable water bottles and glass cups, and using silverware cutlery, instead of disposable items. There are also other ways to reduce the carbon footprint.

Reuse

Before throwing away something in the rubbish bin, perhaps it is possible to donate it to charity, or maybe the other side of that printer paper can be used as scratch paper. Maybe even weave a basket from old newspaper.

Recycle

Buy recycled items whenever possible. With the amount of recycling bins around, recycilng has never been easier!

Appreciate

Nature is the food we eat, the air we breathe, and provides us the habitat to live.
